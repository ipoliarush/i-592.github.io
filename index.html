Game development outsourcing company in Georgia

Syxgame is an outsourcing game development studio with headquarters in Georgia that builds games for external customers all over the world, mostly specializing in regions like Europe, Asia, and the United States.

We provide a full range of game development services that cover game design, art design (2D and 3D), animations, and programming including backend. Our team is a tight cluster of seasoned game developers and artists that are always willing to do the impossible to deliver quality products and ensure maximum satisfaction. That’s why we take pride in creating unique, aesthetically pleasing games that go beyond client expectations and provide true inspiration.
